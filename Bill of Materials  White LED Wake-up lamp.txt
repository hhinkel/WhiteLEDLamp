Bill of Materials
White LED Wake-up Lamp


1. 1 ESP32 DEV Kit V1 microcontroller 
You can get these cheaper if you buy more than 1
2. 1 Strip of White LEDS 5V at a density of 60 leds/m
Make sure that you get 5V lights anything more than that can not be powered without an outside power source
3. 2 Butt end wire connectors 22 ga
4. ~4 inches of 22 ga solid core hookup wire
5. 1 30A 60V N-Channel Power Mosfet TO-220
6. 1 10K Ohm resistor 1/2W
7. 1 32 oz Chinese food container
While I bought these from Amazon you can probably get one from you local restaurant next time you order take out.
8. 1 Empty Toilet paper tube
